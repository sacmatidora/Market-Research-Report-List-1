<p><ul><li>Single Pump Hemodialysis Machine</li><li>Double Pump Hemodialysis Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hemodialysis machine market can be categorized into single pump and double pump machines. Single pump hemodialysis machines utilize one pump to manage blood flow and dialysate, making them simpler and often preferred in smaller or less complex settings. In contrast, double pump machines feature two pumps, allowing for more efficient separation of blood and dialysate circulation, which can enhance treatment effectiveness. Both types cater to different patient needs and clinical environments, impacting their adoption in healthcare facilities.</p></p>
