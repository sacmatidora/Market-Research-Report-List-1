<p><p>The smoking cessation breathalyzer market includes three main types of sensors: electrochemical, pressure, and canister sensors. Electrochemical sensors detect alcohol by measuring changes in an electric current, offering high accuracy and sensitivity. Pressure sensors analyze breath patterns and detect variations in exhaled air pressure, providing indirect measures of substance use. Canister sensors collect breath samples in a sealed environment, allowing for detailed chemical analysis. Together, these technologies support efforts to reduce smoking and improve cessation outcomes.</p></p>
