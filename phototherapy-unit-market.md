<p><p>The phototherapy unit market comprises two primary types: double surface and single surface phototherapy units. Double surface units utilize light-emitting diodes (LEDs) or fluorescent lamps on both the front and back surfaces, providing more comprehensive treatment for conditions like neonatal jaundice. In contrast, single surface units focus their light output from one side, making them simpler and often more cost-effective, but less efficient for certain applications. Both types cater to various healthcare settings, enhancing neonatal care.</p></p>
