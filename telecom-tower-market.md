<p><p>The telecom tower market consists of two primary types: tower structures and mast structures. Tower structures are typically robust, vertical frameworks, often constructed of steel, providing higher elevation and supporting multiple antennas for various service providers. In contrast, mast structures are generally lighter, more streamlined, and designed for specific applications, offering flexibility in deployment. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing telecommunications infrastructure, ensuring extensive coverage, and accommodating the growing demand for mobile services.</p></p>
