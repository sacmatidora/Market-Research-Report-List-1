<p><p>The Motorcycle Anti-lock Braking Systems (ABS) market is divided into two main types: single-channel ABS and multi-channel ABS. Single-channel ABS is primarily designed for rear-wheel braking control, commonly found in entry-level motorcycles, enhancing stability during braking. In contrast, multi-channel ABS manages both front and rear wheels, providing improved overall braking performance and safety across various motorcycle types. This dual control offers riders better handling under diverse conditions, fostering increased adoption and growth in the motorcycle ABS market.</p></p>
