<p><p>Phase Shifting Transformers (PST) are specialized electrical devices used to control the flow of power between different transmission networks by managing phase angles. The market for PSTs is divided into two types: Direct (One Core) PSTs, which utilize a single core to shift phases efficiently, and Indirect (Two Cores) PSTs, which employ two cores to achieve phase shifting, allowing for greater flexibility and control. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing grid stability and optimizing power transmission.</p></p>
