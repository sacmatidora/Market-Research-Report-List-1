<p><p>The automotive window power sunshade market features two primary types: conventional sunshades and LCD sunshades. Conventional sunshades are typically made of fabric or materials that physically block sunlight, providing a straightforward solution for glare reduction and privacy. In contrast, LCD sunshades utilize liquid crystal display technology to adjust light transmission dynamically, offering enhanced control over visibility and heat management. Both types aim to improve passenger comfort and protect interiors, but LCD sunshades bring advanced functionality and modern aesthetics to vehicle design.</p></p>
