<p><p>The industrial plastic pallet market primarily consists of two types: High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE) and Polypropylene (PP). HDPE pallets are known for their strength, durability, and resistance to chemicals, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications and outdoor use. They are often used in the food and pharmaceutical industries. On the other hand, PP pallets are lightweight, flexible, and resistant to moisture, making them ideal for lighter loads and environments requiring easy handling. Both types contribute to efficiency and sustainability in logistics.</p></p>
