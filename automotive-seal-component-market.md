EPDM is favored for its excellent weather resistance and durability, making it ideal for seals exposed to environmental factors. NR offers good elasticity and tensile strength, contributing to performance and comfort. SBR, known for its cost-effectiveness, provides suitable abrasion resistance and flexibility. Each material plays a crucial role in enhancing vehicle performance and longevity.</p></p>
