<p><strong>The Imaging Photometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD-based Imaging Photometers</li><li>CMOS-based Imaging Photometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The imaging photometers market is primarily divided into two types: CCD-based and CMOS-based imaging photometers. CCD-based imaging photometers utilize charge-coupled device technology, offering high sensitivity and excellent image quality, making them ideal for low-light applications. In contrast, CMOS-based imaging photometers leverage complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor technology, providing faster processing speeds and lower power consumption, suitable for real-time imaging. Both types play crucial roles in various applications, including environmental monitoring, quality control, and scientific research, catering to diverse user needs.</p></p>
