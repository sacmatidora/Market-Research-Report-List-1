<p><p>Healthcare Clinical Trial Management Systems (CTMS) can be categorized into three main types: web-based, cloud-based, and on-premise systems. Web-based CTMS are accessible through browsers, enabling easy collaboration and remote access, while cloud-based systems offer scalability and save on infrastructure costs by hosting data on external servers. On-premise systems, conversely, require installation on local servers and offer organizations greater control over their data and processes but come with higher maintenance and upfront costs. Each type serves different operational needs in clinical trials.</p></p>
