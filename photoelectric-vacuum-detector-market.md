<p><strong>The Photoelectric Vacuum Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Photoelectric Effect Detector</li><li>Internal Photoelectric Effect Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photoelectric vacuum detector market encompasses two primary types: external and internal photoelectric effect detectors. External photoelectric effect detectors utilize light exposure from an outside source to trigger a response, making them ideal for applications requiring non-intrusive monitoring. In contrast, internal photoelectric effect detectors rely on light emitted within the system to detect changes, offering enhanced sensitivity and precision. Both types are vital in industrial settings for measuring pressure and vacuum levels, ensuring optimal operational efficiency and safety.</p></p>
