<p><p>Undercounter ice machines are essential for establishments needing ice production in limited spaces. The market features two primary types: air-cooled and water-cooled machines. Air-cooled ice machines utilize ambient air for cooling, making them energy-efficient and easier to install, ideal for dry environments. In contrast, water-cooled machines rely on water for heat exchange, generally requiring more maintenance. They excel in high-temperature settings but may lead to increased water usage, impacting overall operational costs. Each type serves specific operational needs.</p></p>
