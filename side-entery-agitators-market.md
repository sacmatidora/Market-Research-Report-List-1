<p><p>In the side entry agitator market, two primary types are common: single axis and double axis agitators. Single axis agitators are designed for simple, effective mixing and are typically used in applications requiring moderate turbulence. In contrast, double axis agitators feature two rotating axes, promoting more thorough mixing and dispersing of materials. This makes them suitable for high-viscosity fluids or complex blending processes. Both types cater to various industries, enhancing operational efficiency in mixing applications.</p></p>
