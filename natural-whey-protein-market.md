<p><ul><li>Concentrate Form</li><li>Isolate Form</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The natural whey protein market is primarily divided into two types: concentrate and isolate forms. Whey protein concentrate contains about 70-80% protein, retaining beneficial nutrients like fats and carbohydrates, making it a cost-effective and flavorful option. In contrast, whey protein isolate boasts a higher protein content, around 90% or more, with minimal fat and lactose, appealing to those seeking pure protein with fewer calories. Both forms cater to different consumer needs, from fitness enthusiasts to individuals managing dietary restrictions.</p></p>
