<p><strong>The Fuel Tank Sealant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polysulfide Tank Sealant</li><li>Butyl Tank Sealant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fuel tank sealant market is primarily divided into two types: polysulfide tank sealants and butyl tank sealants. Polysulfide sealants offer excellent chemical resistance and flexibility, making them ideal for high-performance applications, particularly in aviation and automotive industries. On the other hand, butyl tank sealants provide strong adhesion and are known for their impermeability to gases and moisture, making them suitable for general fuel containment. Both types are essential for ensuring the integrity and longevity of fuel tanks.</p></p>
