<p><p>Vertical-axis wind turbines (VAWTs) harness wind energy efficiently by utilizing a vertical rotor orientation. The two main types of VAWTs are the Darrieus and Savonius turbines. The Darrieus design features curved blades, optimizing aerodynamic performance for high wind speeds, making it suitable for larger installations. In contrast, Savonius turbines have a simple scoop shape, ideal for lower wind speeds and smaller applications due to their simplicity and robustness. Both types cater to varied market needs from residential to commercial energy solutions.</p></p>
